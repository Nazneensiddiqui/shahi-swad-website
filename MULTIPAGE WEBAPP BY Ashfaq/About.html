Our Story

It all started in 1970 with a dream to start with a Dairy and restaurant by the revered Late Shri Shyam Sundar Harwani. His vision was to highlight Bhopal on the Food map of Madhya Pradesh and excite Bhopali’s heartbeat with super amazing food, sweets and savoury delights. The dream in a very short while went on to become the most treasured and loved sweet shop and restaurant the town ever had.

Manohar Dairy and Restaurant, known for its excellence in quality came up with its first outlet on Hamidia road with the intention of selling dairy products on 15th April 1970 as we were already dealing in supply of milk and milk products in M.P and gradually we moved towards Restaurant and Sweet Business.

In the year 1978 after the sad demise of Mr Shyam Sunder Harwani, the legacy of Manohar Dairy and Restaurant was continued by his sons Mr Manohar Harwani and Mr Murli Harwani. The outlet came into the limelight when started offering a vast variety of contemporary replenishing dishes by our chefs to leave a lasting impact on our guests.50 years later we strive every day to stay true to the promise of serving a delectable pallet of Indian dishes and desserts at pleasantly surprising pricing. For us, our customers are our families.

We want to thank you, from the bottom of our hearts for loving and supporting us throughout.
